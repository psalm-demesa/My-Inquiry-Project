About

Learn more about our mission and values.

What is The Support Space?

The Support Space was created to provide a safe, welcoming, and accessible place where people can find mental health information, support, and resources. Our goal is to help students in Western Springs College - Nga Puna O Waiorea to feel supported, informed, and connected.

Why Mental Health Matters

Mental health is just as important as physical health, and it affects everyone. At The Support Space, we believe that taking care of your mental well-being is essential for living a fulfilling life.

We understand that mental health challenges can be difficult to navigate, and that's why we're here to provide support, resources, and a sense of community. Whether you're struggling with stress, anxiety, depression, or just need someone to talk to, The Support Space is here for you.

What we Offer

  • Mental health information and resources
  • Support groups and peer counseling
  • Inspirational quotes and encouragement
  • Access to professional counseling services
  • Self-help tools and coping strategies

Our Values

At The Support Space, we are guided by the following values:

  • Confidentiality: We respect the privacy of all individuals and ensure that their personal information is kept secure.
  • Accessibility: We strive to make our resources and support available to everyone, regardless of their background or circumstances.
  • Empathy: We approach each individual with understanding and compassion, recognizing the unique challenges they may face.
  • Integrity: We maintain the highest standards of ethical conduct in all our interactions and services.
